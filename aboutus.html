Who We Are

We are Year 7 Group 3 students from EDUSN International School. Our group created this upcycled DIY website as part of our project to promote sustainability and creativity through hands-on learning. We believe that even small actions—like reusing everyday materials—can help make a big difference for our planet.

Our Purpose

This website aims to inspire and educate users about the value of upcycling through creative, hands-on DIY projects. By transforming everyday waste into useful, beautiful items, we promote sustainability, resourcefulness, and environmental responsibility. Our accessible tutorials are especially designed for students, encouraging them to reduce waste, reuse materials, and develop practical crafting skills in a fun and meaningful way.

What We Want to Do

Be Sustainable

We want to help the planet by reusing materials instead of throwing them away.

Be Creative

We love making things that are both useful and cute.

Spread the Word

We hope more people will join the DIY upcycling movement!

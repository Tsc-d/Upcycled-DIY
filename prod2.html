Materials for upcycled scrunchies

Old T-Shirt / Fabric

elasticband

Fabric Glue

Scissors

Safetypins

Needle and Thread

How to Make It

Cut the Fabric: Cut a strip about 18 inches long and 4 inches wide from your upcycled fabric.

Fold and Glue: Fold it lengthwise (inside out) and glue the edge to make a tube. Let it dry completely.

Thread the Elastic: Attach a safety pin to one end of the elastic and push it through the tube.

Secure the Elastic: Knot or glue the elastic ends tightly together so it forms a loop.

Close the Fabric Ends: Tuck in the raw ends and glue or stitch them together to complete your scrunchie.

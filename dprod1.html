How to Make a Cardboard Organizer

Materials Needed

  • Sturdy cardboard
  • Ruler
  • Pencil
  • Scissors or craft knife
  • Strong adhesive (e.g., hot glue or craft glue)
  • Optional: colored paper, washi tape, paint, markers, stickers, double-sided tape

Instructions

Step 1: Plan the Design

Decide what items the organizer will hold and sketch a rough design with measurements.

Step 2: Cut the Cardboard Pieces

Use a ruler and pencil to measure and mark:

  • Base: 10 x 6 inches
  • Sides: Two 10 x 4 inches
  • Front/Back: Two 6 x 4 inches
  • Dividers: Optional, based on your design

Step 3: Assemble the Organizer

Glue the side pieces to the base, then attach front and back walls. Add dividers inside as needed. Let each section dry before continuing.

Step 4: Decorate

Use colored paper, washi tape, paint, or stickers to personalize your organizer. Labels can help with organization.

Step 5: Finish

Allow all glue and paint to dry completely. Then, place your items in the organizer and enjoy!

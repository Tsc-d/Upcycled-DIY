How to Make Bottle Cap Magnets

Materials Needed

  • Clean bottle caps (metal or plastic)
  • Small magnets (flat, strong ones work best)
  • Hot glue gun or strong craft glue
  • Mini photos, stickers, or drawings
  • Acrylic paint or markers (optional)
  • Scissors

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Bottle Caps

Wash and dry your bottle caps. If you want to paint them, do it now and let them dry completely.

Step 2: Decorate the Inside

Cut out small pictures, stickers, or draw something to fit inside the cap. Glue your design in the center and press it down flat.

Step 3: Attach the Magnet

Turn the cap over and glue a small magnet to the back. Let the glue dry completely so the magnet stays secure.

Step 4: Display!

Once dry, your bottle cap magnet is ready to use! Stick it on your fridge, locker, or any magnetic surface.

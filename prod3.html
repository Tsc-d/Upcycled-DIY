Upcycled DIY Project: Paper Bead Jewelry

Old Magazines / Colored Paper

Glue Stick or Liquid Glue

Toothpick / Skewer

String / Elastic Cord

Scissors

How to Make It

Cut Paper Strips: Cut long triangular strips from old magazines or paper (around 1 inch wide at the base, tapering to a point).

Start Rolling: Wrap the wide end around a toothpick and roll tightly toward the pointed end.

Glue It: Add glue to the last 2 inches and finish rolling so it sticks. Hold for a few seconds.

String It: Thread your finished beads onto string or elastic to create necklaces, bracelets, or even earrings.

Wear & Share: Show off your sustainable sparkle or gift them to friends!
